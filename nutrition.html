Healthy Eating Basics

Proper nutrition is essential for maintaining good health and preventing disease.

Key Principles

  • Eat a variety of foods
  • Include fruits and vegetables daily
  • Limit sugar and processed foods
  • Drink enough water

Benefits of Good Nutrition

Balanced nutrition supports the immune system, improves energy levels, and reduces the risk of chronic diseases.
