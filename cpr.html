What is CPR?

CPR is an emergency procedure performed when someone's breathing or heartbeat has stopped.

Basic Steps

  1. Call emergency services immediately
  2. Check if the person is responsive
  3. Push hard and fast on the chest (100–120 compressions per minute)
  4. Give rescue breaths if trained

Why CPR Matters

Immediate CPR can double or triple survival chances during cardiac arrest.

Important: Always seek certified CPR training.
